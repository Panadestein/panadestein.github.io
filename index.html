¡Hola!

My name is Ramón, welcome to my website! I hold a PhD in physics, although the focus of my work has been so far in the field of theoretical and computational chemistry. I have experience in both the development and application of electronic structure and nuclear quantum dynamics methods. On this site, you can find more information about my research activities.

I am very passionate about mathematics and computer science in general. The Emacs text editor is my favorite piece of software, see my literate config. I also happen to write verses sometimes.
